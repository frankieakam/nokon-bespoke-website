Terms & Conditions

Last Updated:

1. Consultation & Booking

All consultations are by appointment only. A 50% deposit is required to secure your booking and begin the bespoke process.

2. Measurements & Fittings

We require precise measurements for optimal fit. Additional fittings may be scheduled if necessary to achieve perfection.

3. Fabric & Materials

All fabrics are sourced from premium suppliers. Once selected, fabric choices cannot be changed after cutting.

4. Delivery Timeline

Standard delivery time is 4-6 weeks from final measurements. Rush orders may incur additional charges.

5. Returns & Alterations

Due to the bespoke nature of our garments, returns are not accepted. Minor alterations within 30 days of delivery are complimentary.
